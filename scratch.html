About Brazil

Brazil is the largest country in South America and is known for its diverse population, natural resources, and cultural traditions.

  1. Key Facts
    • Capital: Brasília
    • Official Language: Portuguese
    • Population: Over 200 million
  2. Geography
    • Amazon Rainforest
    • Atlantic coastline
    • Varied climates and ecosystems
  3. My Opinion on Santa Catarina

      Santa Catarina is a beautiful state in the southern region of Brazil. It is known for its stunning beaches, lush mountains, and vibrant culture. The state is one that has lots of diversity and European influences, particularly from Germany and Italy During my time there, only one crime was reported that happened to missionaries in my mission. This makes me believe that Santa Catarina is one of the most safe states in Brazil, relatively compared to the rest of the country.

Brazilian Culture

Brazilian culture is influenced by Indigenous, African, and European traditions. Music, sports, and festivals play a major role in daily life. A major dance/music style is Capoeira, which combines martial arts, dance, and acrobatics. Brazilians are known as a predominantly Catholic country with vibrant celebrations such as Carnival.
